Instream header-bidding player — live demo

This page shows a header-bidding instream video player on a sample article. It runs a real-time header-bidding auction, plays the winning ad as a pre-roll, then the content video. It is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktop.

Try the sticky player: let the video start, then scroll down — the player shrinks and docks to the bottom-right corner so it keeps playing while you read. Scroll back up and it returns inline; tap the ✕ to dismiss it.
Sponsored Video

The player autoplays muted (required for autoplay on mobile browsers) and plays inline. As you keep reading, the sticky behaviour keeps the impression on screen — which lifts viewability and completed-view rates, the metrics that drive video CPMs.
