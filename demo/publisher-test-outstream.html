Outstream (in-article) player — live demo

This page shows the outstream video unit — built for articles with no video of their own. There is no content video here; the ad slot below stays collapsed until it scrolls into view, then autoplays muted and collapses again when the ad finishes.

Scroll down to the "Sponsored video" slot. As it enters the screen the unit expands and plays muted; tap the speaker control for sound. When the ad ends, the slot collapses and the article closes back up. Fully responsive on mobile and desktop.
